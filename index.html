Arcane

Arcane: League of Legends

a series by Riot Games

Based on the world behind League of Legends, Arcane dives into the delicate balance between the rich, utopian city of Piltover Crest and the seedy, oppressed underground of Zaun Crest. Known across Runeterra as the "city of progress," many of the most brilliant minds call these cities home. But the creation of hextech, a way for any person to control magical energy, threatens that balance. The story follows the origins of two iconic League champions—and the power that will tear them apart. Like the game on which it is based, Arcane is aimed at a "16+" audience and deals with more adult subject matter.

Episodes

Season 1, Episode 01:

Welcome to the Playground

Following a struggle on the Bridge of Progress, the Zaunite sisters Vi and Powder discover their parents dead. Vander, the leader of the failed rebellion, takes them in. Years later, a Piltover penthouse is broken into by Vi, Powder, and their adopted brothers, Mylo and Claggor. After stealing a series of mystical gems, Powder unintentionally breaks one, triggering an explosion that partially demolishes the structure.

Jinx

A chaotic and unpredictable inventor with a flair for destruction. Once a playful girl, she now channels her pain into mayhem, wielding deadly gadgets and an explosive personality.

Vi

A tough, street-smart enforcer with a knack for punching her way through problems. She’s fiercely loyal, with a protective streak for those she loves, especially her sister, Jinx.

Caitlyn

Piltover’s skilled and determined Sheriff, renowned for her sharp intellect and impeccable marksmanship. She’s dedicated to justice and unraveling the city’s darkest mysteries.

Viktor

A visionary scientist striving to merge humanity with technology. His pursuit of perfection blurs the line between innovation and obsession.

Jayce

A charismatic inventor and defender of Piltover’s ideals. Armed with a powerful hammer, he balances his ambition with the weight of responsibility.

Mel

A cunning and sophisticated Piltover councilor with a talent for diplomacy. She’s driven by ambition and her vision for Piltover’s future.

Vander

A protective and compassionate leader of Zaun’s undercity. He’s a father figure to Vi and Jinx, striving to shield his people from harm.

Silco

A ruthless and manipulative leader in Zaun, fueled by a vision of independence from Piltover. His cunning mind makes him a formidable opponent to anyone who opposes him.
